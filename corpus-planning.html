Corpus Planning Strategy

Corpus Planning ensures that your retirement savings are sufficient to meet your future lifestyle goals. At Covea, we help you calculate, build, and protect a corpus that aligns with your retirement objectives.

Future Needs Assessment

We analyze your expected retirement lifestyle, living expenses, healthcare costs, and contingencies to determine the ideal corpus required for a comfortable retirement.

Structured Savings Plan

Based on your current income and risk profile, we create a disciplined savings plan that ensures consistent accumulation towards your retirement corpus.

Investment Mix

Our approach combines equities, bonds, and alternative investments to balance growth potential with risk mitigation, ensuring your corpus grows steadily over time.

Regular Monitoring

We continuously track progress against your retirement goals and adjust contributions and investments to keep the corpus on track.

Inflation Adjustment

We factor in inflation to ensure that your corpus maintains purchasing power over decades, preventing erosion of your future financial security.

Contingency Planning

Our strategy incorporates insurance coverage and emergency funds to protect your retirement corpus against unexpected events or market fluctuations.

Who Should Opt

Corpus Planning is ideal for individuals focused on securing their retirement lifestyle, including salaried professionals, business owners, and high-income individuals seeking structured wealth accumulation.
