Why Income Protection Matters

Income protection ensures financial stability in case of illness, accident, or unforeseen events preventing you from earning. It safeguards your lifestyle and commitments while you recover.

A comprehensive income protection plan minimizes dependency on savings or family support during periods of income loss.
